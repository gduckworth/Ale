My visit to the DXC Digital Transformation Centre helped me and my team clarify what we are trying to achieve with our digital program. Meeting the actual teams was really valuable, as it allowed us to cut through much of the theory and hype associated with digital and better understand the real challenges of implementing and scaling digital on a day-to-day basis. What I found refreshing was that the visit did not really focus on the technology, but much more on the impact on the people and how to implement continuous change.
